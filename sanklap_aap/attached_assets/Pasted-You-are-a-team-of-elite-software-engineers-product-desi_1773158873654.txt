MISSION

Fix India’s broken civic grievance ecosystem by adding an **AI intelligence layer** over government systems.

Delhi has:

• 20 million citizens
• 272 wards
• thousands of civic complaints daily

But today’s systems suffer from:

• complaints disappearing without updates
• duplicate tickets flooding the system
• no SLA enforcement
• officers closing complaints without visiting the site
• confusion between multiple agencies
• language barriers for citizens
• no predictive infrastructure maintenance
• no emergency civic response system

SANKALP AI solves these problems with automation, AI, verification, and transparency.
